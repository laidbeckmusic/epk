Scoring Session — Toronto

Laid Beck writes to picture — cues shaped around tension, drive, and release, built for scenes rather than radio.

Drawing on a production background in house, downtempo, and alt-pop, his scoring work leans cinematic and textural — pads that breathe, rhythm that pulls a scene forward, and restraint where the picture needs space. Full stems and alternate edits available on request for music supervisors and editors.
