Biography

Laid Beck is a Toronto-born producer and multi-instrumentalist crafting warm, cinematic electronic music, pulling from house, downtempo, and alt-pop influences.

His first single "Burning" was immediately signed and has picked up early traction worldwide, blending musicality, ear-candy, and artful songwriting with electronic depth. That instinct isn't new; it's the same one that built a devoted following long before the first Laid Beck record.
